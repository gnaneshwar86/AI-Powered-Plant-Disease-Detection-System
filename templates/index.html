Why Diagnostic Precision Matters?

Plant pathology is vital for securing global food pipelines. Without accurate diagnosis of diseases before symptoms spread widely, pesticide and fertilizer application risks being misdirected, leading to resource waste and environmental damage.

By combining transfer learning CNNs with localizing heatmaps (Grad-CAM), LeafAI enables instant, explainable detection to ensure high yield outputs.

Prevention Best Practices

  • Maintain strong air circulation around plant blocks.
  • Routinely rotate crops to maintain soil nutrients.
  • Inspect leaf undersides prior to greenhouse seeding.
  • Sanitize clipping tools between different rows.
  • Allow root soil to reach proper warm temperatures.
